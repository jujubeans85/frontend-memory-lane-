About Memory Lane

Memory Lane is a Central Coast vintage hunt — part curated shop, part treasure recovery unit.

We buy, sell, and source pieces with good bones: mid-century lighting, cane and timber, sideboards, textiles, and the weird little objects that make a home feel alive.

If you’ve got something to move on, we’ll keep it simple and fair. If you’re chasing something specific, send a photo and a rough budget — then we’ll go digging.
